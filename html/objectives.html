Objectives of this Project

  1. Why this software?
OBJECTIVES: This project was initiated from an inward inspiration from the authors to help eleviate the stress that first year in pharmacy poses. Though this does not completely make the course stress free, it goes a long way to make studies easier. In pharmacy most of the things you study are related in one way or the other and hence studies should be carried out as such. Try enough to relate the things you study. This project therefore helps you out in this direction since it gives you an easy access to almost all your study materials.

ADVICE FROM ISAAC AMPONSAH

Congratulations on your successful admission to Kwame Nkrumah University of Science and Technology and for that matter the Faculty of Pharmacy and Pharmaceutical Sciences. The Doctor of Pharmacy programme is one of the very few prestigious programmes that one can read in this university. A lot of people desired it but weren't fortunate enough to get the opportunity that luck has brought your way. You must thus see yourself special as you begin your training as a Doctor of Pharmacy.

"Pharmacy school has never been easy" - that was what we were told upon our arrival into this faculty and my guess is that you may have already heard it by now. It is very true but we were more determined than to let what they said scare us. We only did what was required of us as pharmacy students and were able to survive the strong waves of the first year.

Colleague students, although lectures will be very tiresome; being in class from 8:00am to 5:00pm daily, lecturers keep keep coming and going, having book-like handouts to read, the content of which you may or not understand, having numerous deadlines to beat as the assignments keep flooding your way and having lots of things to read as you prepare for daily pre-lab quizzes, let not these things weary you out nor scare you. Envisage yourself in the future in your white lab coat, saving lives with the knowledge you acquire now and be motivated to endure these storms.

Dear friend, with this software at your disposal now, read your handouts always ahead of the lecturer, desire desperately for high scores in your lab quizzes and all examinations, ask questions, visit the library, find out all the things you are asked to find out either in class or in the lab, study to understand and apply what you understand, and you will surely become a very competent 'friend of the human race'
